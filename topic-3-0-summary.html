STUDY TIPS

TIP #1- REVIEW YOUR NOTES! Review all of the important information in this module including: astronomer's names, dates, theories and laws.

TIP #2 - SYSTEMIZE YOUR NOTES! Use color coded note cards, a box, 3 ring binders, spiral notebooks, or a combination of these methods to keep your notes organized.

TIP #3 - REFLECTIVE CONVERSION OF YOUR NOTES - As you study, reflect on your notes, considering how you would answer in your own words a description, summary, or comparison of the concepts on within your notes.

TIP #4 - RE-VISIT ALL LINKED SITES!! Those sites were not added for a reason!! Take time to look through ALL the content!

TIP #5- Click here to visit the UTSA Tomas Rivera Center for additional study tips and don't forget to study with your classmates or ask them for help. You are all a HUGE resource for each other!
