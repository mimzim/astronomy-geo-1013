The target audience is UTSA undergraduate students in a large scale earth sciences class.
We will build an online astronomy module of the solar system that will meet the instructorâ€™s need for a supplemental, individual, on-line course, providing interactivity to a segment of the course the instructor sees as dull in a lecture setting.


This astronomy module will provide an overview of the bodies that make up our solar system. Additionally, the module will take a closer look at Earth, to include Earth-sun systems, Earth-moon systems, and Earth seasons. Learners will benefit by actively engaging with the content, in addition to passively participating in a classroom lecture.


The product will be published to Google Code, and will be an open-source product available to the general public for use and modification.
