Big Idea

His first law says that planets move in elliptical orbits around the sun.


First to describe a sun-centered solar system.


His observation of Jupiter's moons and Venus' phases lent support to a heliocentric solar system model.


Introduced the idea of epicycles: planets move IN circular orbits ON circular orbits around the Earth.


He used the law of gravity to explain the speed and shape (ellipse) of planetary orbits.


His 20 years of data helped Kepler support a heliocentric model of the solar system.
