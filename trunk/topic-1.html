Navigating the Course

The course is made up of two main topics with subtopics under each heading. Once you have completed a topic, the next topic becomes visible. At the end of each main topic, there will be a self-check. If you miss the question, you may go back to the topics to review. After the entire course has been navigated, the final quiz will become available to take. If you receive a passing grade, you may save and print your certificate of completion. Gook luck!
